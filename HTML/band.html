Sanam is an Indian pop rock band based in Mumbai consisting of Sanam Puri (Lead Vocals), Samar Puri (Lead Guitar and Vocals), Venky S or Venkat Subramaniyam (Bass Guitar and Vocals) and Keshav Dhanraj (Drums and Vocals). They are also referred to as India's Fabulous Four. The band’s business manager Ben Thomas, Founder of Kurian & Co. Talent Management, is often referred to as the fifth band member. Together they strive to create the sound & style the band is recognised for by blending 4 different musical influences. This band is considered to bring back the band culture and independent music into India after a notable gap.SANAM band is India's first music artist to cross 1 Million Subscribers on YouTube.The band is known for being amongst India’s top 10 independent YouTube Channels India’s biggest music artist on the digital platform and the fastest growing YouTube Channel in the country

The Local Train is an Indian rock band from Delhi, India. Formed in Chandigarh Tricity in 2008, the band has been recognized for its active role in cultural festivals in Indian colleges.Sennheiser ranked The Local Train as India's No. 1 rock band in 2015.Their debut album "Aalas Ka Pedh" was released in September 2015, and the band's YouTube videos have collectively received over a million views as of April 2016.

Underground Authority is an alternative rock / rap rock band from India. Formed in early 2010, in Kolkata, their music is flavoured by a blend of protest poetry, reggae, alternative rock, rap rock and hard rock. The band is noted for their socio-political messages / Anti-capitalism agenda in their song lyrics and also for their Music to express and not impress quote.
